厦门海傲斯科技有限公司
Xiamen Helios Technology Co., Ltd
  • 移动互联网带来的快捷、方便影响着越来越多人的生活，悄然的改变着人类的生活方式。以手机为终端，穿梭于虚拟和现实之间。厦门海傲斯科技有限公司基于此背景于2018年成立，是一家致力于研究虚拟现实，研发增强人体感受设备的公司，拥有授权和再审专利共18项，其中发明专利6项。
  • 公司根据居家、户外运动、旅行、车载、电竞等不同的应用场景研发出了虚拟现实座垫、背包、休闲椅、电竞椅，基本满足了不同的年龄段、不同的职业人士对虚拟现实产品的幻想。
  • 公司的研究方向是在人体对外认知的听觉和视觉基础上，增加感觉，让人更能沉浸于虚拟环境中，体验身临其境。
  • The fast and convenient brought by mobile Internet affects more and more people‘s lives and quietly changes the way of human life. Mobile phones are used as terminals to shuttle between virtual and reality. Based on this background, Xiamen Helios Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 2018. It is a company devoted to the research of virtual reality and the development of human sensory enhancement equipment. It has 18 patents of authorization and retrial, including 6 patents of invention.
  • According to different application scenarios, such as home, outdoor sports, travel, car-borne, electronic competition, the company has developed virtual reality cushions, backpacks, leisure chairs and electronic competitive chairs, which basically meet the fantasies of different age groups and different professionals about virtual reality products.
  • The company's research direction is to increase the sense on the basis of human body's external cognition of hearing and vision, so that people can be more immersed in the virtual environment and experience immersion.
